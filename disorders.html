Common Disorders

Urinary Tract Infection (UTI)

Symptoms: Painful urination, frequent urge to urinate, cloudy urine.

Causes: Bacterial infection, poor hygiene, urinary retention.

Treatments: Antibiotics, increased hydration, proper hygiene.

Kidney Stones

Symptoms: Severe pain in back or side, blood in urine, nausea.

Causes: Dehydration, high salt intake, certain medical conditions.

Treatments: Pain relievers, drinking water, medical procedures.

Chronic Kidney Disease

Symptoms: Fatigue, swelling, changes in urination.

Causes: Diabetes, high blood pressure, kidney damage.

Treatments: Medication, lifestyle changes, dialysis.

Incontinence

Symptoms: Involuntary urine leakage, frequent urination.

Causes: Weak pelvic muscles, nerve damage, infections.

Treatments: Exercises, medication, surgery.
