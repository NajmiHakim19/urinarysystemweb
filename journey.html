Urine Formation Journey

Click each process for more details

Filtration
Blood is filtered in the glomerulus to remove waste and excess substances.
Reabsorption
Useful substances like glucose and water are reabsorbed back into the blood.
Secretion
Additional waste substances are secreted into the tubules.
Excretion
Final urine is excreted into the ureters and transported to the bladder.
